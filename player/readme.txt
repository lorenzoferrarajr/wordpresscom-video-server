WordPress Video Player
======================

Contributors: Automattic Inc  
Tags: WordPress video, video solution
Requires at least: 2.5
Tested up to: 2.7

== Description ==
This is the video player developed at Automattic Inc and used at wordpress.com.

It was written in actionscript 3. The player takes an argument, video guid, then queries a predefined
server for video XML information.  Then it displays and plays the video. 
Eg, http://your_domain/wp-content/plugins/video/wpp.swf?guid=OUzHUPL9

To Test: 
Load /public_html/wpp.html or simply click the /public_html/wpp.swf to play the video.
